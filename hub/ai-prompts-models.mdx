## Seller context

**File:** `src/prompts/context.py`

The `SELLER_CONTEXT` block is injected into every AI prompt to ground responses in Profound's business:

- **Company:** Profound — AI Search Visibility Platform (AEO/GEO)
- **Funding:** $58.5M raised ($35M Series B, Sequoia Capital, August 2025)
- **Customers:** 500+ including Ramp, U.S. Bank, Rippling, MongoDB, DocuSign
- **Products:** AEI, Prompt Volumes, Agent Analytics, Agents/Workflows, Shopping, Citations
- **Key stats:** 100M+ daily queries, 4.4× conversion rate vs organic

## Company intel

**Model:** Claude Opus | **File:** `src/prompts/company_intel.py`

Synthesizes web-scraped content and Snowflake firmographic data into a structured company profile.

**Modes:**

- `full` — generates all profile sections
- `news` — updates only `recent_news` and `hiring_signals`
