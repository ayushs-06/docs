Account Intelligence Hub is an internal web application for Profound's Sales and CS teams. It unifies Salesforce accounts, Gong call intelligence (via Snowflake), and AI-generated company research into a single interface.

<Note>
  This is an internal tool. Access requires a valid Profound WorkOS SSO session.
</Note>

## What it does

The Hub runs AI agents powered by the Anthropic API to generate:

- **Company profiles** — web-scraped via Firecrawl and enriched with HubSpot firmographic data
- **Call summaries** — structured summaries of Gong calls with topics, decisions, objections, and sentiment
- **Action items** — automatically extracted from call summaries
- **Loss analyses** — post-mortem AI analysis for Closed Lost accounts
- **Meeting prep briefs** — tailored briefs by meeting type (discovery, demo, negotiation, and more)
- **Health scores** — computed from Snowflake `DAILY_ENTERPRISE_*` tables (currently disabled)
